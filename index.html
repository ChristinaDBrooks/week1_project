Gitty Travellers

Like a Phoenix rising out the ashes, houseless but not homeless, Gitty Travellers band members formed from the seedy underbelly of Detroit, creating an eclectic sound style of dynamite guitars, evasive rhythm, sticky vocals, combining artisanal songwriting and sugarmanly stage presence. Influenced by the sounds and counterculture of the sixties, Gitty members owe some of their inspiration to Pink Floyd and have gone on to influence other bands such as Nickelback. Gitty Travellin' band plays tunes from a variety of genres and have been swashbuckling elbows in venues around the world since 1969. Watch for Gitty Travellers coming to your local Death Valley this Spring!
